Where it all started

Established in 2007 by Kari Pagle and Jenny Roland from Telemark, Norway. We now employ 7, spread around the world. We were innovators in building products that are environmental friendly, and animal cruelty free. We never went into industrial design methods. We kept our tradition with making handcrafted chairs.

What the future brings

Looking foreward, we are seeking and research into ways to use new, innovative and mindfull materials that helps keep our carbon footprint to a minimum. Due to this mindset, we have 1 full time employee that is working on new ways to make faux leather, to make our chairs stand out in comfort.
